Read & Write Differently

Who said books need to be linear?

Why does a book need to have a definitive path from start to end? Why not have several path navigating through a story. This is what WebNovel aims to do. By giving users the ability to create alternative chapters or brand new paths, its goal is to give the opportunity for anyone to collaborate on a novel.

Don't like a chapter? Create your own

If you find a chapter you don't like there is no need to worry. You or other members can create an alternative chapter so that others can enjoy your version as well.
